About Us

Each year, Stony Brook Motorsports designs, builds, tests, and races an off-road vehicle, competing against hundreds of colleges around the world in the Baja SAE competition. Team members can develop skills in design, manufacturing, electronics, and business. All Stony Brook students, regardless of year, major, or experience, are welcome to join.
